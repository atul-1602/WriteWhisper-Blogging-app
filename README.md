# WriteWhisper-Blogging-app
WriteWhisper is a modern and feature-rich blogging application that allows you to easily create, manage, and share your thoughts and ideas with the world.



## Features

- **User-Friendly Interface**: An intuitive and responsive user interface for a seamless writing and reading experience.

- **User Authentication**: Create your account, log in, and manage your profile with ease.

- **Create and Edit Posts**: Write, edit, and format your blog posts using a powerful and user-friendly editor.

- **Categories and Tags**: Organize your posts with categories and tags for better content discovery.

- **Commenting System**: Engage with your readers through a built-in comment system.

- **Search Functionality**: Easily find specific posts or topics of interest.

- **Responsive Design**: Ensuring a consistent and enjoyable experience across devices.

- **Draft Saving**: Save your work as drafts to come back to later.

- **Social Sharing**: Share your posts on social media platforms.

- **User Profiles**: Customize your profile, showcase your posts, and interact with other writers.

## Technologies Used

- Frontend: React, HTML, CSS
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- User Authentication: JSON Web Tokens (JWT)
